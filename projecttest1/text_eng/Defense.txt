Corruption in the sector of Defense can manifest in the following ways:
1.Financial Mismanagement:
◦Embezzlement of Budgetary Funds: Fraudulent handling and embezzlement of funds during the implementation of various military programs and projects.
◦False Reporting: Submitting falsified reports on the use of financial resources.
2.Abuse in Military Procurement:
◦Fake Tenders: Manipulating tender results and awarding contracts to preferred companies without genuine competition.
◦Bribery and Compensation: Accepting bribes for awarding or modifying military procurement contracts.
3.Misuse in Supply of Defense Equipment:
◦Fake Supplies: Fraudulent activities in the supply of defense equipment, including the delivery of defective or substandard equipment.
◦Bribery and Compensation: Accepting bribes for signing or modifying contracts related to the supply of military equipment.
4.Abuses in Personnel Policy:
◦Nepotism: Appointing individuals to positions or extending their terms without proper competition or meeting relevant requirements.
◦Unjustified Appointments: Making appointments and promotions without appropriate qualifications.
5.Mismanagement of Military Service Conditions:
◦Illegal Service Bonuses: Providing illegal bonuses and incentives to personnel in exchange for bribes.
◦Misuse of Government Support: Misuse of support programs intended for military personnel (e.g., medical assistance, social support).
6.Abuses in Public Property and Procurement:
◦False Property Registrations: Falsifying property records and engaging in illegal transactions related to defense assets.
◦Bribery for Property Acquisition: Accepting bribes for the acquisition and use of defense property.
7.Abuses in International Cooperation:
◦Bribery for International Programs: Accepting bribes in the context of international defense programs and projects.
◦Illegal Agreements: Entering into illegal or false agreements with international partners.
8.Misuse in Defense Personnel Functions:
◦Illegal Training: Falsifying defense personnel training programs and illegal exploitation.
◦Bribery for Training: Accepting bribes for training or appointment to positions.
9.Abuse of Military Information:
◦False Information: Providing falsified information about military situations or defense programs for personal gain or bribes.
◦Leakage of National Security Information: Illegally disseminating critical national security information.
10.Lack of Public Oversight:
◦Lack of Accountability: Absence of accountability and transparency in the Ministry of Defense's operations.
◦Lack of Public Oversight: Insufficient public oversight and participation in the implementation of programs and projects.
